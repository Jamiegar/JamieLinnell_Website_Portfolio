Covide20

Role:

Technical Design & Programming

Game Description:

This game is a top-down 4X style game. The player starts the game in a world where a messenger informs the player that the world has been kill by the virus known as Covide20. The player must fight infected bandits trying to reach the city. The main objective is to find the cure for this virus by sending out units to explore the world and collect items. A combination of these items will enable the kingdom to create the cure and stop all of humanity from certain extinction.
