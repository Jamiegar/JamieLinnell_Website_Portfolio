Covide20

This project was completed in my second year of university. Our task was to create a Game that was as close to being finished as we can and even publishable. I had chosen to create a hex-tile top down management game. I took huge inspiration from Civilization 6. When the player starts the game they are in a world where everyone has been killed by the virus know as Covide20 they must fight agents the odds by using units to explore the world to collect Items to find the cure, while infected bandits try to infect the city. If the players population is all killed by the virus they lose. However if the player is successful and finds the items to create a cure they win!
